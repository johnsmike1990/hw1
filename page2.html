Ibanez Jem7VWH

The Mona Lisa of Guitars

History

The Ibanez JEM Series pays tribute to guitar virtuoso Steve Vai. The JEM7VWH might be the most recognizable instrument of the series since Steve Vai has been playing versions of this guitar for more than a decade. This is the flagship of the Ibanez line; you will not be disappointed by the incredible playability and screaming tone.

The Self Gift

Mike purchased this guitar for himself as a reward for getting into Kellogg. This is his primary guitar which he uses for playing several genres, from heavy metal to twangy country. While this guitar basically never goes out of tune, unfortunately it is unable to be tuned to anything besides standard EADGBE tuning.

Specifications

The JEM7VWH features the following specs:

  • Alder body for clear, full-bodied tone
  • 24-fret Prestige neck
  • 5-piece maple/walnut neck
  • Rosewood fingerboard
  • Edge Pro bridge
  • 3 DiMarzio Evolution pickups
  • Pearl/abalone vine inlays
  • Gold hardware
