About Mike

Mike has been an avid musician for most of his life, even semiprofessionally at times. While he has long since hung up his dreams of rock stardom, he has never let the passion die down. Even today, he shreds at least an hour a day, and plays guitar in the Kellogg Rock Band called the Rocket Pockets.

Musical Influences

Mike's musical stylings are super eclectic. He is just as likely to be jamming out to some classic Led Zeppelin or Black Sabbath as he is to be tapping his toe to some 90s country or today's pop music. If it has a beat, it has value.
