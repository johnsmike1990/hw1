Gibson Montana

Tone for Ages

History

Introducing the new Gibson Montana J-45. For the first time ever, Gibson Acoustic is proud to offer their cornerstone model with a newly designed cutaway body shape. Famous Vintage Sunburst finish. The iconic finish, each hand sprayed for an authentic Gibson Acoustic look. The new J-45 Cutaway is spec’d just like the J-45 Standard. Including Nickel Grover tuners, black 1.725” nut, Tusq saddle, mahogany back and sides, plus the famous Gibson Vintage Sunburst finish. For the 2017 model features the added versatility of an LR Baggs VTC pickup for easy plug and play anywhere you perform.

Mellowing Out

This addition to the collection was actually driven by Mike's wife. Tired of hearing him blast through Metallica and Megadeth nonstop, she encouraged him to get a guitar that would be complementory to some less frantic music. Another great thing about this guitar is that is can be plugged in electrically; something that will definitely come in hand for shows at Kellogg this year!

Specifications

The Montana features the following specs:

  • Body Style: J-45 Cutaway
  • Wood: Sitka spruce top, mahogany back and sides
  • Binding: Multi-ply top, single-ply back, multi ply single-ring rosette
  • Finish: Vintage Sunburst, nitrocellulose lacquer
  • Neck: Mahogany, slim taper profile with 1.725” black nut
  • Fingerboard: 12” radius rosewood, mother-of-pearl dot inlay
  • Electronics: L.R. Baggs Element VTC
  • Pickguard: 0.60’’ Tear Drop
