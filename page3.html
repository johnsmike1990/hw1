Fender American Stratocaster

The Original

History

The Fender American Professional Stratocaster solidbody electric guitar has been crafted to give you the best design elements from classic vintage Strats and the most popular modern features in one gig-ready instrument. Tim Shaw designed the three V-mod single-coil pickups for a vintage voice that is balanced across all the switch positions. Fender's popular neck carve has been enhanced to a Deep-C profile that fits your hand comfortably. The Fender American Professional Stratocaster has all the elements that make Fender guitars legendary, and much more.

Versatility

The wonderful thing about a strat is its versatility. You can get so many different sounds with this thing, and the feel is just unbeatable. The axe is so perfectly contoured to fit a human body that it is a wonder how right they got this thing when it was originally designed in the 50s. This is Mike's primary gigging guitar, as there is just no beating the sound quality and comfortability.

Specifications

The Strat features the following specs:

  • A Stratocaster design that blends the best of modern and vintage construction
  • Balanced-sounding alder body
  • Maple neck with comfortable deep "C" profile
  • 3 Shaw-designed V-mod single-coil pickups are balanced across the positions
  • Narrow-tall frets that are the most requested size in the Fender Custom Shop
  • 2-point tremolo with pop-in arm
  • Comfortable 9.5" fretboard radius
  • Chrome hardware
